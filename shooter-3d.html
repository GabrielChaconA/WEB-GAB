Python Ursina Engine 3D FPS Survival

SHOOTER-3D

An action-packed 3D first-person shooter (FPS) survival game built in Python using the **Ursina Engine**. Battle against waves of iconic creatures (Creepers, Skeletons, Endermen, Wardens, and Withers) in a modular codebase.

Gameplay & Combat Mechanics

SHOOTER-3D is an intense survival experience where you face scaling waves of voxelized creatures (Creepers, Skeletons, Endermen, Wardens, and Withers). Each enemy features customized AI and unique attack patterns: Creepers approach silently to self-destruct, while Wardens and Withers fire devastating projectiles.

As a player, you must manage your resources, replenish your ammunition by collecting supply boxes spawned across the map, and perform high-mobility maneuvers to dodge incoming attacks.

  • Stamina-Based Movement: Sprinting consumes your stamina, forcing you to strategically manage your speed boosts during critical combat situations.
  • Tactical Ground Slide: Execute quick ground slides to dodge under projectiles or escape tight corners when cornered by enemies.
  • Scaling Difficulty: Enemies receive scaling damage, movement speed, and health multipliers as you survive through consecutive rounds.

⚔️ Game Modes

ROUNDS (Normal)

Survive consecutive waves of enemies with scaling difficulty. Early rounds spawn basic creatures to let you adapt, while higher rounds gradually introduce elite monsters.

HARD MODE

The ultimate challenge. Spawns all elite monsters (including Withers and Wardens) right from the start in high quantities. Absolute chaos.

Game Controls

W A S D Movement
SHIFT Sprint (consumes stamina)
C Slide (quick slide on ground)
LEFT CLICK Shoot / Fire Weapon
ESC Quit Game Immediately

Installation & Requirements

Prerequisites: Python 3.8 or higher installed on your system.

1. Install the required dependencies:

pip install -r requirements.txt

2. Run the main script from the root directory:

python src/main.py
