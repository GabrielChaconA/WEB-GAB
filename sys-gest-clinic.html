SYS-GEST-CLINIC

A comprehensive and premium digital solution designed to optimize and automate schedule control, patient records, and real-time operational reports in physical therapy and rehabilitation clinics.

View GitHub Repository ← Return to Portfolio

Project Purpose & Context

SYS-GEST-CLINIC was developed to support healthcare small and medium enterprises (SMEs) in transitioning towards full digital transformation. Many medical practices still rely heavily on paper logs or flat, disconnected spreadsheets prone to duplication and human error.

This solution introduces a centralized, high-performance database coupled with a sleek, minimalist frontend. The platform focuses on security, speed, and real-time operational efficiency to manage complex clinical schedules, patient profiles, and medical records seamlessly.

  • Collision-Free Agenda: An automated validation algorithm prevents overlapping appointments across treatment areas and specialist schedules.
  • Automated Ledger tracking: Instantly updates patient balances and records medical fees according to the therapies or rehab consultations scheduled.
  • BI Dashboards: Synthesizes clinical metrics (attendance ratios, popular sessions, and individual specialist performance) into clear, actionable charts.
  • Role Integration: Unifies administrative management and medical roles into a single secure access workflow.

Modules & Technical Capabilities

The application consists of five key architectural modules that guarantee system integrity and user ergonomics:

Profiles & Security

Access control with roles for Clinic Directors and Therapists. Employs Bcrypt password hashing and features webcam integration for live avatar capture and cropping.

Patient Files

Maintains history records, insurance plans, referring physician notes, specific clinical details, and aggregates account balances dynamically.

Smart Calendar

Schedules standard clinical consultations, aquatic sessions, and manual therapies. Includes dynamic collision triggers to block overbooking in real-time.

BI operational Stats

Processes check-in/out records into charts. Displays treatment distributions, specialist metrics, and clinical efficiency reports.

Dynamic PDF Export

Allows doctors and administrators to generate structured, professional PDF documents. Features an interactive modal to customize the data to be exported (such as complete medical history, notes, or patient progress summaries), ready for printing or digital sharing.

Local Orchestration

The services are containerized to eliminate version conflicts and ease deployment workflows.

Compile and spin up containers in detached mode:

docker-compose up --build -d

Note: This automatically initializes the local PostgreSQL database (sysgest_db), maps backend REST APIs on port 8001, and serves the frontend on port 5174. Initial SQL seed structures are run automatically.
